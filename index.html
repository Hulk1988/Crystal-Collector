Crystal Collector!

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, and you will lose if your total score goes above the random number as well.
  • The value of each crystal will be hidden from you until you click on it and see your score change.
  • Each time a new round starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
